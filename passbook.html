Take a deep breath, you're okay. Press "Forgot Password" in the app's login screen. Choose a security question that you have answered previously and enter submit. If you forgot your security answer, hit "Forgot Answer". An email with a code will be sent to the email address you entered when you first logged in. Your Password, Email, and Security Questions can all be updated once you are logged in by tapping the settings button in the top left corner.

By Email:

Tap on the settings tab and hit "Backup". An Email will be created with a file containing the backup. Hit send and you're done!


By iCloud:

Tap on the settings tab and hit the button next to "Use Sync". This will upload your settings and passwords to iCloud.

By File:

If you have a file as your backup (it should have the extension .xml), tap and hold the file on your phone until a screen appears with a list of apps to open in. Select Passbook to restore your backup.


By iCloud:

If you previously backed up your passwords on iCloud using the Sync method, make sure you are logged into the safe iCloud account on your phone and then turn on Sync again. All your passwords will be restored and your current passwords will be merged with them
